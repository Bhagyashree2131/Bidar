PAPNASH TEMPLE


                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                   

papnash temple description

Papnash Shiva Temple is one of the most popular temples of the city. It is believed that Lord Ram while returning from Lanka installed a Shivalingam in this temple.

Owing to its importance, the temple is always flocked with devotees from all over the country especially during the festival of Shivratri. The surroundings of the temple 
are also quite captivating with a natural spring flowing into a pond Papnasha. A cycle-rickshaw or an auto-rickshaw is one of the best medium of commutation to the temple 
from city center.
